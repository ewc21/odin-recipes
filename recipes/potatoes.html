Yum yum tomatoes

Ingredients

  • potatoes
  • wood
  • grass
  • salt
  • butter

Directions

  1. cut potatoes into large chunks
  2. add some grass to the potatoes
  3. preheat oven to 380 celsius
  4. add salt to wood, make sure to season well
  5. put the wood in the oven for 50 hours
  6. add potatoes to wood, then serve while warm
